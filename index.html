What a wonderful place to visit.

Carribean Cuisine

food was amazing, the seafood was fresh and the fruits were sweet. I had the best time eating my way through the island.

They have a sweet bread called johnny cake that is to die for. I also had the best rum punch ever.

The Beaches

The beaches were beautiful. The water was clear and the sand was soft. I had the best time swimming and snorkeling.

I also went on a boat ride and saw some of the most beautiful coral reefs.

The People

The people were so friendly and welcoming. I felt right at home. There were so many breathe taking views from high places.

I also learned a lot about the culture and history of the island. The islands are mostly mountains, so when people are walking down, strangers will offer you a ride to the top or bottom, how cool is that?.
